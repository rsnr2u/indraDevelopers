## ğŸ”§ How to Change Colors

### Step 1: Go to Admin Panel
1. Login at `/admin/login`
2. Navigate to **Settings** from sidebar
3. Click on **Theme Colors** tab

### Step 2: Choose Your Colors
You'll see 4 color pickers:

#### **Primary Color** (Main brand color)
- Use for your main brand identity
- Recommended: Bold, vibrant color
- Example: `#701939` (Maroon/Burgundy)

#### **Secondary Color** (Supporting color)
- Should complement primary
- Usually darker or lighter shade of primary
- Example: `#4a0e24` (Dark Maroon)

#### **Accent Color** (Highlight color)
- For highlights and emphasis
- Can be same family or contrasting
- Example: `#a02850` (Light Maroon)

#### **Text Color** (Headings)
- Main heading text color
- Usually dark gray or black
- Example: `#1f2937` (Dark Gray)

### Step 3: Save Changes
1. Click **"Save Settings"** button
2. Theme updates **automatically** across the entire website!
3. No page refresh needed - colors change instantly
